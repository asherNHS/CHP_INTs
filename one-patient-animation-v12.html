Help shape the role of Clinical Health Psychology in the Westminster INT

As the government expands Integrated Neighbourhood Teams (INTs), we are seeking partner views on mental health input. We are specifically interested in the role of Clinical Health Psychology within your INT.

We are, therefore, inviting clinicians and experts across the Octopus network and the wider healthcare system to share how Clinical Health Psychology could best operate within the Westminster INT.

What is Clinical Health Psychology? Clinical Health Psychology supports people who experience psychological difficulties linked to physical health problems. It brings together care for the mind and body by providing person‑centred, evidence‑based support across hospital and community services. The work takes a wide view, considering biological, psychological, and social factors that influence health and recovery throughout the care journey.
